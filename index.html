FAQ

1. What exactly do you offer?

+

We offer custom website and system development, fully responsive, focusing on performance, usability, and your brand identity.

2. What is the average delivery time for a website?

+

It depends on the project type. A simple institutional site can be delivered within 7 to 15 days. More complex projects may take 20 to 30 days.

3. How does the creation process work?

+

After understanding your idea, we create a draft, develop the structure and design, program the functionalities, and conduct testing. Additionally, you can follow the project's progress through our exclusive system, where you’ll receive an access code to view real-time updates and send feedback until the final delivery.

4. What is the cost of a website?

+

The price depends on features, number of pages, and level of customization. Our projects start at 10,000 MT and vary according to the scope.

5. Do you offer support after delivery?

+

Yes. We offer free support for 15 days after delivery. We also have monthly support plans for maintenance, updates, and improvements.
